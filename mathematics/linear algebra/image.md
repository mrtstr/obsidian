### image of a [[function]]
- if $x \in A$ then $y=f(x) \in B$ is the **image** of $x$ and $x$ is the **preimage** of $y$
$$
\forall x \in A
$$
- the **image of function** is a [[set]] containing the images for all elements of $A$ is a subset of $B$
$$
\begin{split}
im(f) 
&= f(A) \\
&= \{f(a): a \in A\} \\
&\subseteq B
\end{split}
$$
