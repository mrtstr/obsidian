### n fold cross validation
- divide the [[empirical distribution of a dataset|dataset]] into $k$ subsets of equal size
- train the model on $k-1$ subsets and evaluate on the left out set
- repeat that for all $k$ subsets
- the final result is the mean out of all subsets
