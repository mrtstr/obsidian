### catesian product
$$
\bigtimes_{i=1}^n A_1 = \left\{(a_1, ..., a_n)\: : \: \forall i \in [n]: a_i \in A_i \right\}
$$
- the [[catesian product]] of $n$ [[set|sets]] is a [[set]] of [[n tuple|n tuples]] with a [[cardinality]] of $\prod_{i \in [n]} |A_i|$
