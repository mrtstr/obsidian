### positive definite [[function]]
- a [[function]] $f: A \rightarrow B$ is [[positive definite]] on a neighbourhood $D$ of the [[origin]] if the following is true
$$
f(0) = 0 \land \forall v \in D: f(v) \geq 0
$$
