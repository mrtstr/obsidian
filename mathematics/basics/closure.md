### closure
- a [[set|subset]] $H \subseteq V$ is closed under an operation $\circ$ if the following condition is true
$$
v, w \in H \rightarrow v \circ w \in H
$$
- example $(\mathbb{Z}, +)$ is [[closure|closed]] but $(\mathbb{Z}, :)$ is not
