## [[lhospital rule]]

$$
\begin{split}
\lim_{x \rightarrow c} \frac{f(x)}{g(x)} = \lim_{x \rightarrow c} \frac{\frac{df(x)}{dx}}{\frac{dg(x)}{dx}}
\end{split}
$$
