### homogeneous
- a [[function]] $f: V \rightarrow W$ is [[homogeneous]] with degree $k$ is the following is true
$$
\forall v \in V, \lambda \in \mathbb{K}: \lambda \neq 0: f\left(\lambda v\right) = \lambda^k f\left(v\right)
$$
