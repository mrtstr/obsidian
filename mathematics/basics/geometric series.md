### geometric series
geometric [[series]] is converging for $0< q < 1$

$$
\sum_{k=0}^\infty q^k = \frac{1}{1-q}
$$

- [[geometric series]] for [[matrix|matrices]]
$$
\sum_{i=0}^{n-1} A^k = (I-A)^{-1}(I-A^n)
$$
