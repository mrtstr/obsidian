## Definition
[[logarithm]] with [[eulers number]] as the basis $\ln(x) = \log_e(x)$

## properties
$$
\lim_{h \rightarrow 0} \frac{\ln(1 + h)}{h} = 1
$$
## [[limit]] identity of [[natural logarithm]]
$$
\ln(a) = \lim_{h \rightarrow 0} \frac{a^h - 1}{h}
$$
