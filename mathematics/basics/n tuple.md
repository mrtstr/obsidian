### n tuple
- ordered collection of $n$ elements
- can be defined as a [[set]] of [[set|sets]]
- can be defined as a [[set]] or ordered pairs

$$
\begin{split}
(a_1, ..., a_n) 
&= \left\{ \{a_1\}, \{a_1, a_2\},..., \{a_1, ..., a_n\} \right\} \\
&= \left\{ (1, a_1),..., (1, ..., a_n) \right\} \\
\end{split}
$$
