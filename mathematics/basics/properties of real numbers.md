# basic properties of [[real number|real numbers]]
all other properties can be derived from the following
## [[associative]]
$$
\begin{split}
&\forall a,b \in \mathbb{R}: \\
&a + (b + c) = (a + b) + c \\
&a \cdot (b \cdot c) = (a \cdot b) \cdot c \\
\end{split}
$$
## [[commutative]]]
$$
\begin{split}
&\forall a,b \in \mathbb{R}: \\
&a + b = b + a \\
&a \cdot b = b \cdot a \\
\end{split}
$$

## [[distributive]]
$$
\begin{split}
&\forall a,b,c \in \mathbb{R}: \\
&a \cdot (b+c) = a \cdot b + a \cdot c \\
\end{split}
$$
## [[neutral element]]
- $0$ is the [[neutral element]] of the [[addition]]
- $1$ is the [[neutral element]] of the [[multiplication]]

$$
\begin{split}
&\forall a \in \mathbb{R}: \\
&a + 0 = 0 + a = a \\
&a \cdot 0 = 0 \cdot a = a \\
\end{split}
$$
## [[inverse elements]]
### [[addition|additive]] inverse
For all $a \in \mathbb{R}$ exists a [[addition|additive]] inverse $a'$ for which the following condition is true:
$$
\forall a \in \mathbb{R} \: \exists a': a + a' = 0
$$
$$
a' = -1 \cdot a
$$
### [[multiplication|multiplicative]] inverse
For all $a \in \mathbb{R}$ except $0$ exists a [[multiplication|multiplicative]] inverse $a'$ for which the following condition is true:
$$
\forall a \neq 0 \in \mathbb{R} \: \exists a': a \cdot a' = 1
$$
$$
a' = \frac{1}{a}
$$
