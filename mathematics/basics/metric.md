## Metric
â†’ Function measuring distance between points in a metric space $d(x, y)$
### Properties
1) $d(x,y) \ge 0$ (positive)
2) $d(x,y)=d(y,x)$ (symmetry)
3) $d(a,c) \le d(a,b) + d(b,c)$ (triangle inequality)

- can be induced by a [[norm]]: $d(x, y)= \|x-y\|$
