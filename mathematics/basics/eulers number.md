## [[series]] definition of [[eulers number]]
$$
e = \sum\limits_{k=1}^\infty \frac{1}{k!}
$$
$$
e^\lambda = \sum\limits_{k=1}^\infty \frac{\lambda^k}{k!}
$$

## [[limit]] definition of [[eulers number]]

$$
e = \lim_{n \rightarrow \infty} \left(1 + \frac{1}{n}\right)^n
$$
