### derivative of a constant
$$
\begin{split}
f(x) &= c \\
\frac{df(x)}{dx} &= 0
\end{split}
$$
#### proof

$$
\begin{split}
\frac{df(x)}{dx} 
&= \lim_{h \rightarrow 0} \frac{f(x + h) - f(x)}{h} \\
&= \lim_{h \rightarrow 0} \frac{c - c}{h} \\
&= 0 \\
\end{split}
$$
