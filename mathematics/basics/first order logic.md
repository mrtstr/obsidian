#### logic connectives
- negation $\neg A$
- conjunction $A \land B$ (A and B)
- disjunction $A \lor B$ (A or B)
- implication $A \rightarrow B$ 
- equivalence $A \leftrightarrow B$
- exclusive disjunction $A \nleftrightarrow B$
