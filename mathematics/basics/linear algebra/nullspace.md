# Nullspace or Kernel

of [[matrix]] $A$

## Definition

$$
kernel(A)=\left\{ x \in \mathbb{R}^{\mathcal{\left|J\right |}} \mid Ax=0 \right\}
$$
## Properties
- $range(A) \subseteq\mathbb{R^{\left| \mathcal{I} \right| \times \left| \mathcal{J} \right|}}$ (is [[subspace]] of $\mathbb{R^{\left| \mathcal{I} \right| \times \left| \mathcal{J} \right|}}$)
- $dim(kernel(A)) = corank(A)$ (the dimensions of the [[nullspace]] are equal to the [[corank]])
