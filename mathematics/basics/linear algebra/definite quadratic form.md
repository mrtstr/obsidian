# Definition
A [[square matrix]] $A \in \mathbb{R}^{\left| \mathcal{I} \right| \times \left| \mathcal{I} \right|}$ is 
## positive definite
1) $x^\top Ax > 0$   
2) $\lambda_i(A) > 0$ (all [[eigenvectors|eigenvalues]] $>0$)
3) [[sylvesters criterion]]

## semi positive definite
1) $x^\top Ax \geq 0$   
2) $\lambda_i(A) \geq 0$ (all [[eigenvectors|eigenvalues]] $>0$)
3) [[sylvesters criterion]]

## negative definite
1) $x^\top Ax < 0$   
2) $\lambda_i(A) < 0$ (all [[eigenvectors|eigenvalues]] $>0$)
3) [[sylvesters criterion]]

## semi negative definite
1) $x^\top Ax \leq 0$   
2) $\lambda_i(A) \leq 0$ (all [[eigenvectors|eigenvalues]] $>0$)
3) [[sylvesters criterion]]

# Properties
1) $A$ is positive definite iff $A$ has a [[cholesky decomposition]]
2) $A$ is positive definite iff $\left< x,y\right> = x^\top A y$ defines a [[dot product]]  
3) if a [[hessian matrix]] is [[definite quadratic form|definite]] helps to determine [[stationary point]] is a [[maxima and minima|minimum or maximum]]
