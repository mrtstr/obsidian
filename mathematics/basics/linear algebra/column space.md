# Column Space
- or image or range of a [[matrix]]

## Definition
- for a matrix $A  \in \mathbb{R^{\left| \mathcal{I} \right| \times \left| \mathcal{J} \right|}}$ the [[column space]] $range(A)$ the set of all linear combinations of its column vectors $A_{(j)}$ 

$$
range(A)=\left\{ Ax \mid x \in \mathbb{R}^{\mathcal{\left|J\right |}}\right\}
=span\left(\left\{A_{(j)}\right\}_{j \in \mathcal{J}}\right)
$$
## Properties
- $range(A) \subseteq\mathbb{R^{\left| \mathcal{I} \right| \times \left| \mathcal{J} \right|}}$ (is [[subspace]] of $\mathbb{R^{\left| \mathcal{I} \right| \times \left| \mathcal{J} \right|}}$)
- $dim(range(A)) = rank(A)$ (the dimensions of the [[column space]] are equal to the [[rank]])
- $dim(range(A)) = dim(rowspace(A))$ (the [[column space]] and the [[row space]] have the same number of dimensions)
