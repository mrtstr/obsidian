# Matrix
- A matrix $A\in \mathbb{R^{\left| \mathcal{I} \right| \times \left| \mathcal{J} \right|}} \left(\in \mathbb{R}^{m \times n}\right)$ induces a [[unique]] [[linear map]] $F(x)$ that maps the $\mathbb{R}^n$ to its [[column space]] $Im(A) = Span\left((A_{(j)})_{i \in \mathcal{I}}\right) \in \mathbb{R}^{m}$
$$F(x) = Ax\in \mathbb{R}^{m \times n}: \mathbb{R}^{n} \mapsto Im(A) \in \mathbb{R}^{m}$$
## Definition
$$
\begin{split}
A  \in \mathbb{R^{\left| \mathcal{I} \right| \times \left| \mathcal{J} \right|}} 
&= \left(a_{i,j} \right)_{i \in \mathcal{I}, j \in \mathcal{J}} \\
&= \left(A^{(i)} \right)_{i \in \mathcal{I}}
= 
\begin{pmatrix}   A^{(1)} &   ... & A^{(\left| \mathcal{I} \right|)}  \end{pmatrix}\\
&= \left(A_{(j)} \right)_{j \in \mathcal{J}}
=  
\begin{pmatrix}   A_{(1)} \\   ... \\ A_{(\left| \mathcal{J} \right|)}   \end{pmatrix}
\end{split}
$$
