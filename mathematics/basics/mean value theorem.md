## Theorem
Let $f$ be a [[continouse function|continouse]] [[derivative|differentiable]] [[function]] in the interval $(a,b)$ then the following is true
$$
\exists c \in (a, b): \left.\frac{df(x)}{dx}\right|_{x=c}=\frac{f(b)-f(a)}{b-a}
$$
