[[stochastic independent|stochastic independents]] between $\left\{A_i \mid i=1,...,k\right\}$ that only occurs when an [[event space|event]] $B$ happened
## Definition


$$P\left(\bigcap\limits_{i=1}^{k} A_i \middle\vert B \right) = \prod\limits_{i=1}^{k} P(A_i \mid B) $$
