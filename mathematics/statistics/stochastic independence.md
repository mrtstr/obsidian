In a [[probability space]] two [[random variable|random variables]] are [[stochastic independence|independent]] if their representing [[set|sets]] in the [[event space]] are [[disjunct]]

## Properties
1) $A \perp B$
2) $P(A \cup B) = P(A) + P(B)$
3) $P(A \cap B) = P(A)P(B)$
4) $f_{AB} = f_A(a)f_B(b)$
