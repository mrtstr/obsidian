### laplace experiment
- given a [[probability space]] $(\Omega, \mathcal{A}, P)$ with a finite and [[countable]] [[sample space]] $\Omega$ 
- the corosbonding [[random experiment]] is a [[laplace experiment]] if the following is true
$$
\forall \omega \in \Omega: P(\omega) = \frac{1}{|\Omega|}
$$
- for a [[laplace experiment]] the probability of a [[event]] $A$ is given as
$$
P(A) = \frac{|A|}{|\Omega|}
$$
