## Definition
$$
\begin{split}
0! & =  1 \\
n! &=  \prod\limits^{n}_{k=1} k \\
&= n \cdot (n-1)!
\end{split}
$$
## Complexity
$$
\mathcal{O}(k^n) \in \mathcal{O}(n!) \in \mathcal{O}(n^n)
$$

## Applications
1) [[binomial coefficient]]
2) number of [[permutations]]
3) [[sterling number]]
