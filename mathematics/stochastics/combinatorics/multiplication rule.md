Given [[random experiment]] with $k$ different parts with $n_1, ..., n_k$ outcomes. The [[cardinality]] of the [[sample space]] is given by the product of the outcomes of the parts

$$
|\Omega| = \prod\limits_{i=1}^k n_i
$$
