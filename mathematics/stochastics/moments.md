# moments
the $kth$ [[moments|moment]] of a [[random variable]] $X$ is defined as the [[expectation]] of the absolute of $X$ to the power of $k$.
$$
\mathbb{E}\left[|X^k|\right]
$$
# central moments
the $kth$ central moment of a [[random variable]] $X$ is defined as the [[expectation]] of the difference between $X$ and the mean of $X$ to the power of $k$.
$$
\mathbb{E}\left[(X- \mu)^k\right]
$$
# moment generating function

$$
\psi(t) = \mathbb{E}\left[e^{tx}\right]
$$
