[[vector notation]] for $n$ dimensional [[random variable]] and its [[joint distribution]]
$$
\begin{split}
&\boldsymbol{X}=(X_1,...,X_n)\\
&f_\boldsymbol{X}(\boldsymbol{x})=f_{X_1...X_n}(x_1,...,x_n) \\
&F_\boldsymbol{X}(\boldsymbol{x})=F_{X_1...X_n}(x_1,...,x_n)=P(X_1 \leq x_1 \: \cup \: ... \: \cup \: X_n \leq x_n) \\
\end{split}
$$
