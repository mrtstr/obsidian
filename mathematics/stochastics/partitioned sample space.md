The partition of a [[sample space]] is a [[set]] of [[event space|events]] $B = \{B_i \mid i=1,2,...,k\}$ that is 
1) $\forall i\neq j \in [1,...,k]B_i \cap B_j =\emptyset$ ([[mathematics/basics/disjoint]])
2) $\bigcup_{i=1}^{k} B_i=\Omega$ (the [[union]] over all $B_i$ is equal to the [[sample space]] $\Omega$)
