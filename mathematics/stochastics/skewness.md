The [[skewness]] of a distribution is a measure of the lack of symetry of the [[probability density function (PDF)]] / [[probability function]] of a [[random variable]]. 

$$
\frac{\mathbb{E}\left[(X-\mu)^3\right]}{\sigma^3}
$$
