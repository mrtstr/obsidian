
## Definition
used for 
- [[sterling number]]
- [[gamma function]]

$$
\begin{split}
\Gamma(\alpha)
&=\int\limits^\infty_0 x^{\alpha-1} e^{-x} \\
\end{split}
$$
## for $\alpha \in \mathbb{N}$ 
$$
\begin{split}
\Gamma(\alpha)
&=\int\limits^\infty_0 x^{\alpha-1} e^{-x} \\
&=(\alpha - 1) \Gamma(\alpha -1) \\
&=(\alpha - 1)! \Gamma(1) \\
&=(\alpha - 1)! \\
\end{split}
$$
