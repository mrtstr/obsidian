- given a [[random variable]] $X$ with a [[statistical model]] $(\mathfrak{X}, \mathcal{A}, \mathbb{P}_\vartheta: \vartheta \in \Theta)$
- given an unbiased [[statistical estimator]] $S$ for a part of the parameters $\gamma(\vartheta) \subset \vartheta$ 
- for a [[measurable function]] $T:\mathfrak{X} \to \mathfrak{Y}$ the [[conditional expectation]] has the following properties

$$
S^*_\vartheta = \mathbb{E}[S | T]
$$
