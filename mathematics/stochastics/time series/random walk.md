### random walk
- [[time series]] model where each value is equal to the previous values plus a the [[random variable]] $S_t$ that is one step in a random direction (in the two dimensional case its a [[bernoulli distribution|bernoulli random variable]])

$$
X_t = X_{t-1} + S_t
$$
