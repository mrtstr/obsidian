### black scholes model
- [[black scholes model]] describes
- [[wiener process]] $W_t$ the
- $P_t$ is a geometric brownian motion
- drift parameter $\mu$ and the [[volatility]]
$$
P_t = \exp\left(\left(\mu - \frac{\sigma^2}{2}\right)\cdot t + \sigma W_t\right)
$$
