### martingal
- [[stochastic process]] with zero [[drift]] and finite [[expectation]]
- [[conditional expectation]] of all future values gives everything that happend in the past is equal to the current value

$$
\begin{split}
&\mathbb{E}[|X_{t}|] < \infty \\
&\mathbb{E}[X_{t+h}| X_1, ..., X_t] = X_t
\end{split}
$$

- a [[stochastic process]] $Y_t$ is [[martingal]] with respect to another [[stochastic process]] $X_t$ if the following is true

$$
\begin{split}
&\mathbb{E}[|Y_{t}|] < \infty \\
&\mathbb{E}[Y_{t+h}| X_1, ..., X_t] = Y_t
\end{split}
$$
