### Definition
- [[expectation|expectation]] of the squared deviation of a [[discrete random variable]] from its mean
- measure of how for a [[discrete random variable]] spreads out

$\mathbb{VAR}[X]=\mathbb{E}[(X-\mathbb{E}[X])^2]=\mathbb{E}[X^2]-\mathbb{E}[X]^2$

(variance of a set of [[observations]] see [[sample variance]])

### properties
- $\mathbb{VAR}[a]=0$ and $\mathbb{V}[X+a]=\mathbb{VAR}[X]$ 
- $\mathbb{VAR}[X+Y]=\mathbb{VAR}[X]+\mathbb{V}[Y]+2 \mathbb{COV}(X,Y)$  (with [[covariance]] $\mathbb{COV}[X,Y]=0$ when $X$ and $Y$ [[independence|independent]])
- $\mathbb{VAR}[aX]=a^2\mathbb{VAR}[X]$
