## Discrete Case
$$
\begin{split}
&f_X(x) = \sum\limits_{all \: y} f_{XY}(x,y) \\
&F_X(x) = \sum\limits_{x_{min} }^{x}\sum\limits_{all \: y} f_{XY}(x,y)
= P(X \leq x, y \leq \infty)\\
\end{split}
$$

## Continuous Case
$$
\begin{split}
&f_X(x) = \int\limits_{-\infty}^{\infty} f_{XY}(x,y) dy \\
&F_X(x) = \int\limits_{-\infty}^{x}\int\limits_{-\infty}^{\infty} f_{XY}(x,y) dy dx = P(X \leq x, y \leq \infty) \\
\end{split}
$$
