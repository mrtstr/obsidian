### exponential distribution
#### [[probability function]]
- $f_X: \mathbb{R}_{\geq 0} \to \mathbb{R}$

$$
f_X(x) = \lambda e^{-\lambda x}
$$
#### [[expectation]]
$$
\mathbb{E}[X] = \int_0^\infty x \lambda e^{-\lambda x}
$$
