The [[standard deviation]] of a [[random variable]] $X$ is the nonnegative square root of its [[variance]] $\mathbb{VAR}[X]$ if the [[variance]] exists.

$$
\sigma = \sqrt{\mathbb{VAR}[X]}
$$
